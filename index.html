About TPCC

Toronto Persian Cycling Community

    TPCC is a welcoming cycling community focused on safe group rides, good vibes, and making new friends. We’re open to all ages, backgrounds, and experience levels.

Our Goal

Safety • Respect • Community

    Create a friendly, inclusive space for cyclists to connect.
Organize as many rides as we can and encourage consistent participation.

Promote safe, respectful riding and strong group-ride etiquette.
